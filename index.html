Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for school leaders, teachers, and families.

Is this a gaming event or a learning event?

It is a structured learning event. Students build projects, learn Lua, and present outcomes. Recreational gameplay is not part of the schedule.

When do group projects begin?

Students split into project groups on Day 3 (Tuesday), then continue collaborative production through Saturday showcase day.

What does the optional hotel choice cost?

Students can choose to stay at home or in the optional hotel arrangement for a $10 fee per student.

How do schools monitor progress?

Schools receive daily summaries of milestones, participation, and project status from the program team.
